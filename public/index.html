§ 02 · Who runs it

One person,
one obsession.

I'm Julian. Salsa dancer, host, and the guy who started keeping a list of Medellín's best nights because nobody else was.

Rumba began as a weekly text I'd send to friends ·where to dance Tuesday, who's playing live Saturday, which club to skip. It got long. It became a guide. The guide became a brand, and the brand became a way to introduce the scene I love to the people who want to find it ·locals first, visitors second.

Today there's the events guide, the Cali trip with my friend Janusz from Switzerland, and a small tienda on the way. Same hands behind all of it.

Julian
